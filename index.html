About Me 👋🏻

👋🏻 I am a Master's student at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI&DS), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) , working at the SCAI LAB and advised by Prof. Jingrun Chen and Prof. Congcong Zhu.
